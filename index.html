Cinema Ticket App

This platform was built for an independent cinema specialising in Bollywood block-busters. Users can navigate a real time event schedule and select available movies, dates and showtime before making a booking.


The dynamic webpage behaviour was developed using event driven background AJAX requests. Fetching the most up-to-date data from the DB & inserting it into the DOM with no page reloading.

Workshop Event Feed

Co_Lab is a small collective who hold regular workshops and events in a multi-venue community space. Their website acts as a notice-board where hosts can provide details and publicise upcoming events.


This custom made full stack application consists of a dynamic feed, which can be filtered by venue and a simple but secure 'back-end' C.M.S, where staff can draft/add/edit & remove posts.
